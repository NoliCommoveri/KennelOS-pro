Furever

Send a family their pup's own free care app — KennelOS Furever — seeded with its name, birthday, your contact, and a lifelong vaccine/care schedule. One tap opens the app already set up; a resend (say the pickup date slips) just updates it in place.

Kennel identity

Copied into every seed link you send — your kennel name, your own contact, and your vet's contact (a referral for the family before they have their own). Edit any time; it applies to the next link you send.

Pups to send

One entry per pup with an open sale. Add an optional personal note and pickup details, then Prepare link to build the seed link and hand off a real email / SMS you tap to send.
